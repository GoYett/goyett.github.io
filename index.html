The Problem

What problem buyers face today

  • Lack of reliable digital access to authentic Indian offline retail
  • Poor trust in quality when buying from India online
  • No one to verify products in‑person
  • High risk of fakes & wrong items
  • Heavy international shipping complexity

They want trust, curation, quality verification, and real shopping from real stores. No platform solves this at scale.

Categories shoppers ask for

Local crafts Bridalwear Jewellery Handicrafts Festive shopping Gifting Regional boutiques

Yett Global Concierge

Your personal shopper from Indian stores, offering a live, curated, authentic experience

  • Live agent visits verified stores & artisans
  • Video call, photos, and chat for selection & quality checks
  • Escrow‑protected checkout; no‑return, fully traceable flow
  • Transparent invoices + AI dashboard for comparative pricing (flags >20% deviation)
  • End‑to‑end global shipping: duty, tracking, support managed by Yett

How it works

  1. The Shopper chooses city & categories (Lucknow, Jaipur, Varanasi & more)
  2. Sees local crafts, curated stores
  3. Selects a store from the list or via product search
  4. Books an agent with an optional shopping note → appointment scheduled
  5. Agent visits store → video/photos & chat for selections
  6. Shopper confirms → pays into escrow
  7. Yett buys, packs, and ships globally → duty & tracking handled
Checks & Safeguards: Escrow, AI‑based price deviation flags, transparent bills, full traceability.

Why Now

Structural tailwinds

  • 35M+ overseas Indians; 5.4M in the USA alone; high disposable income & cultural nostalgia
  • Post‑Covid comfort with remote, video‑led shopping and concierge services
  • Shift from e‑commerce to curated commerce, especially for high‑value purchases
  • White space: no scaled solution combining offline Indian retail + live human selection + global delivery
  • Cross‑border commerce growing >20% YoY; Tier‑2 digitization accelerating where Yett has on‑ground presence

Yett’s readiness

  • 3,000+ stores onboarded; deep Tier‑2 networks
  • Content engine (blogs, reels) and BD network already running in 5 indian cities
  • Consumer app + merchant tools
